MEASURE UP

Measure spaces and

More Details
xi
With Measure Up, you can measure spaces and objects around you using WebXR.

To launch Measure Up, please visit this site using the Chrome browser on an Android device.
You can use Measure Up to take quick measurements of the spaces and objects around you in augmented reality. Measurements may vary slightly from device to device, and should be taken as a guide.

Measure Up uses the WebXR APIs built into Chrome, and runs entirely in your browser. All data processing and space mapping happens in your browser and no data ever leaves your device.
